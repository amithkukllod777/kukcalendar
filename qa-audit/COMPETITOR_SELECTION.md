# Competitor Selection — Kuk Calendar

- **Category:** Personal calendar app (Android), offline-first, with account cloud-sync and light task/reminder features.
- **Target users:** Individuals & SMB users, India + GCC focus; part of the Kuklabs ecosystem (single account across KukBook/KukTask/KukKeep).
- **Business model:** Free app, value = ecosystem sync (one Kuklabs account). No paid tier in-app today.
- **Platforms:** Android (Flutter). No iOS/web/desktop client yet.
- **Checked:** 2026-07-14.

## Selected competitors
| # | Competitor | Type | Why relevant | Evidence confidence |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| C1 | **Google Calendar** | Market leader / direct | Pre-installed on most Android; the default users compare against; free; sharing, recurring, tasks, widgets, offline. | VERIFIED (Play listing + Google support, 2026-07-14) |
| C2 | **any.do (To-do & Calendar)** | Emerging challenger / closest model | Same calendar-*plus*-tasks-*plus*-reminders-*plus*-sync bundle Kuk Calendar aims at; freemium; multi-view. Best analogue to Kuk's product shape. | VERIFIED (any.do site + Play, 2026-07-14) |
| C3 | **Samsung Calendar** | Close substitute / OEM default | Default calendar on the largest Android install base in Kuk's target markets (India); the "do nothing / use built-in" alternative. | INFERRED — general capabilities well-known; specific 2026 features NOT VERIFIED (no dedicated source check this pass) |

**Not selected (and why):** Outlook (different segment — enterprise/email-centric); TimeTree (group-sharing niche); Business Calendar 2 (power-user paid niche). Popularity alone was not used as a selection criterion.

## Evidence rules applied
Every competitor feature in the matrices is labelled VERIFIED / PARTIALLY VERIFIED / INFERRED / NOT VERIFIED with the date checked (2026-07-14). C3 (Samsung) rows are INFERRED/NOT VERIFIED pending a dedicated verification pass. No outdated screenshots were used as proof of a current feature.
